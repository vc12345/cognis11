Module 33: Hidden Words

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A hidden word spans the "seam" between two adjacent words. It is formed by the last few letters of the first word and the first few letters of the second word. To solve these, you must stop seeing the words and start seeing the string of letters as a continuous line.

Advanced Year 2Find the 3-letter word hidden in: "THE ATOM".
Average Year 4A hidden word exists in: "FISH ARE DEEP". Can you find it?
Advanced Year 5Find the 4-letter word hidden in: "HE MADE A GOAL".

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Meaning" Trap: Your brain naturally wants to read the words "THE" and "ATOM" for their meaning. This "blinds" you to the letters in the middle.

The Fix: Use your finger to cover the first half of the first word and the second half of the second word. By isolating the Seam, the hidden word (EAT) will pop out immediately.
