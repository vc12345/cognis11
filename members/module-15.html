Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Decimal" Trap: Students often write 2 hours and 30 minutes as "2.3 hours."

The Fix: Always remember that **0.5 hours is 30 minutes**, not 50. When doing column addition for time, if the minutes column reaches 60, you must "carry" 1 to the hours column and reset the minutes.
