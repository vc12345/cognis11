Module 17: Reverse Operations

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: Reverse Operations (or Inverse functions) require us to work backward from a known result to a hidden starting point. The key is to **reverse the order** of events and **invert the operations** themselves (e.g., + becomes -).

Entry"I add 5 to my number and get 12. What was it?"
Standard"I multiply by 2 then add 10 to get 30. Find the number."
Advanced"I square a number, add 4, then divide by 2 to get 10. Find the number."
