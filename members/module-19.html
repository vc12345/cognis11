Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Linear" Trap: Reading an equation like a sentence.
Example: $4 + 2 \times 3$. Type 1 thinkers get $18$ ($6 \times 3$). Type 2 thinkers get $10$ ($4 + 6$).

The Fix: Powers and Roots are "heaviest." Multiplication/Division are "heavy." Addition/Subtraction are "light." The scale always processes the heavy operators first, no matter where they sit!
