Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Face Counting" trap. Students often count every visible square face they see, forgetting that a single block can have 1, 2, or 3 visible faces.

The Fix: The Column Method. Instead of counting faces, count the "skyscrapers." Look at the top-most blocks. If a block is 3 levels high, write down "3." Move to the next column. Sum the column heights at the end to get the perfect total.
