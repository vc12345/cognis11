Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Taking the total (100) and dividing by 2 (50), then adding the gap (50 + 20 = 70).
The Error: 70 + 50 = 120. You've broken the total!
The Fix: (Sum - Difference) ÷ 2. Subtract the gap first to find the equal parts.
