Module 18: Probability

THE "COMPLEMENTARY" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker calculates the chance of something happening when the question asks for the chance it doesn't. A "Type 2" thinker always reads the word NOT carefully.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "The chance of winning a race is 1/5. What is the chance of NOT winning?"
2. "The probability it rains is 0.3. What is the probability it stays dry?"
3. "A bag has 3 red and 7 blue marbles. Find the probability of NOT picking red."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

The Law of 1:
All probabilities in a scenario must add up to 1.
Probability of NOT = 1 - (Probability of IT).
