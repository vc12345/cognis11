Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Blind Total" Trap: Students see a number and a ratio, and out of pure habit, they add the ratio parts together and divide the number by the total (e.g., dividing the difference by 8). Always identify the anchor: Does the number represent the Total, or the Gap?
