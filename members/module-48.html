Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Picking a net where side faces are detached from the base edges.

The Fix: The Perimeter Match. Count the base sides. Every side must share an edge with a wall face in the final form. If the base is an octagon, you need 8 rectangles or 8 triangles.
