Module 15: Time Conversions

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: Time calculations do not follow the standard decimal system (Base-10). Instead, they use Modular Arithmetic (Base-60). The core challenge is managing the "reset" point where 60 minutes transforms into 1 hour.

Entry (Year 2)"Convert 140 minutes into hours and minutes."
Standard (Year 4)"A film starts at 15:30 and is 115 minutes long. When does it end?"
Advanced (Year 5)"A clock loses 4 minutes every hour. If it is set correctly at 9am, what time will it show at 9pm?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Decimal" Trap: Mistakenly thinking that 1.5 hours is 1 hour and 5 minutes.

The Fix: Always remember that in time, 0.5 = 30. Half of the 60-minute container is 30, not 50. Use the tanks to visualize the 60m boundary!
