Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Linear Limit." Assuming that only vertical or horizontal lines produce opposites.

The Fix: The 1-Step Jump. On any net, if you move one square and jump over the next, that third square is the opposite. Even in "Z" or "Stair" nets, this jump rule identifies 90% of the pairs.
