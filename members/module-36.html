Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Look-Alike" Trap: Thinking DESSERT and DESERT are anagrams because they look similar.

The Fix: Count the letters. DESSERT has two 'S's; DESERT only has one. An anagram must be an exact 1:1 inventory match. If there is one extra letter, the "sorted key" will not match, and they are NOT anagrams.
