Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "First Slot" Trap: Students often try to put the stolen letter only at the beginning or end of the second word.

The Fix: The letter can hide anywhere inside the second word. For example, if you move 'R' from 'CHART' to 'EAT', it doesn't just make 'REAT' or 'EATR'—it makes 'EART' (not a word) or 'ARET'... but if you move the 'H', it makes 'HEAT'.
