Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Trying to memorize the list as you read it. In multi-step clues, your brain will often "swap" the positions of the middle items because it lacks a physical anchor.

The Fix: The Spaced Anchor. Draw a line. Place the first person in the middle. Place everyone else as a "Neighbor" to that person. Let the line expand naturally as you fill the gaps.
