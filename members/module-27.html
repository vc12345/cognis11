Module 27: Perimeter

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Perimeter is the distance around the outside. Think of it as a fence around a field.

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Rule: Add all sides. If it's a rectangle, it's $2 \times (\text{Length} + \text{Width})$.

Perimeter: cm

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Missing Side" Trap: Only adding the two sides shown in a diagram and forgetting the other two!
