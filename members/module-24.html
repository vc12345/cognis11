Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Average of Averages" Trap: Trying to solve the problem by averaging the current average with the new target (e.g., if current mean is 10 and target is 13, guessing the missing score is 16 because 13 is midway).

The Fix: Mean problems are hidden Total problems. Always calculate: (New Count × New Mean) - (Old Count × Old Mean). The difference between these two totals is always your missing value.
