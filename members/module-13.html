Module 13: Train & Tunnel Problems

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A train passing a point only travels its own length. To clear a tunnel, it travels the tunnel length PLUS its own length.

Entry"A train 100m long passes a post at 20m/s. How long does it take?"
Standard"A train 200m long travels through a 400m tunnel at 72km/h. How long until it clears?"
Advanced"A train takes 15s to pass a pole and 45s to pass a 600m tunnel. Find the train's speed."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Front-Only" Trap: Students often calculate time for the front to reach the exit. But the train isn't "clear" until the tail leaves.

The Fix: Distance = Length of Train + Length of Tunnel.
