Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Distance Drift." Students often put the reflected hole in the wrong part of the quadrant (e.g., reflecting a corner hole into the center).

The Fix: Mirror Invariance. If the hole is 1cm from the fold line, its reflection **must** be 1cm from the fold line on the other side. Use the crease as a ruler, not just a line.
