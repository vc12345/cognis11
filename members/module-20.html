Module 20: Range & Spread

THE "GAP" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker assumes Range is an average. A "Type 2" thinker knows Range is a measure of consistency—the gap between the best and the worst.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "Find the range of the following temperatures."
2. "How much did the scores vary?"
3. "Work out the difference between the highest and lowest values."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

The Formula:
Highest Value - Lowest Value = Range.
