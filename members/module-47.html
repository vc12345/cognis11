Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Touching Edge" fallacy. Assuming two squares that share a corner on the flat net will be adjacent on the cube.

The Fix: Use the 1-Step Jump. On a 2D net, move one square, jump over the next, and the third square is your opposite. These two faces will never touch on a 3D cube.
