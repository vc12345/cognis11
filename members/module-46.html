Module 46: Series Progression

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A series is a visual equation. To find the missing piece, you must isolate every attribute (Position, Rotation, Shading) and determine its unique mathematical rule. The final frame is simply the sum of all these rules applied one last time.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Visual Overload. Trying to track three moving parts at once causes the brain to "average" the movement, leading to a guess.

The Fix: Feature Isolation. Use your finger to physically block out everything except the small dot. Find its rule. Write it down. Then block the dot and look only at the main shape. Never look at the full picture until the very last second.
