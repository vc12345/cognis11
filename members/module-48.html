Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Forgetting the "Base." Students often focus on the side flaps and ignore that the bottom face might be a different shape (e.g., a square base for a pyramid vs. a triangular base).

The Fix: The Perimeter Check. Count the edges of the base. That is exactly how many flaps must be attached to the net. If there are fewer or more, the net is a fake.
