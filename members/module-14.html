Module 14: Interval Logic

Phase 1: The X-Ray

In 11+ exams, "Interval Logic" tests your ability to count the spaces between objects. The number of gaps is always one less than the number of items.

Entry"A clock strikes 4 times. There are 3 seconds between each strike. How long from the first to last strike?"
Standard"A straight fence is 20m long. Posts are placed every 5m. How many posts are needed in total?"
Advanced"A doctor gives a patient 5 pills to take every 30 mins. How long until all pills are taken?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Zero-Start" Trap: Students often forget that the first item (post or chime) happens at "Time Zero." There is no gap before the first chime.

The Fix: Always sketch a quick diagram. If there are 5 objects, draw 5 dots and count the spaces. You will see there are only 4 spaces. $Gaps = N - 1$.
