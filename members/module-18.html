Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Left-to-Right" Trap: Treating math like reading a book.
Example: $10 - 2 \times 3$. A Type 1 thinker does $10-2=8$, then $8 \times 3 = 24$.

The Fix: Use the Priority Scan. Before solving anything, scan for the "Heaviest" operator. Multiplication and Powers are physically "heavier" and crush lower operators into waiting.
