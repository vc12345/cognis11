Phase 1: The X-Ray

Reverse Percentages ask you to find the Original Price. You are at the destination; you need to drive back to the start.

Adv Year 2"A half-price toy costs £5. What was the full price?"
Avg Year 4"After a 10% discount, trainers cost £45. What was the original price?"
Adv Year 5"A house worth £240,000 has increased in value by 20%. What was it worth before?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Forward Percent" Trap: If a price is £90 after a 10% discount, students often find 10% of £90 (£9) and add it back to get £99.

The Fix: Percentages are not "fixed amounts." 10% of the original is more than 10% of the sale price. You must use Division by the multiplier to undo the change.
