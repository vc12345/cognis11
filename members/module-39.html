Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Inversion" Trap: Getting the relationship right but the order wrong. If the first pair is Part:Whole, the second cannot be Whole:Part.

The Fix: Always use the "Sentence Check." If you can say "A Hand HAS A Finger," you must be able to say "A Foot HAS A Toe." If you say "A Toe HAS A Foot," the bridge has collapsed.
