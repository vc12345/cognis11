Module 5: Direct Proportion (Scaling)

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Direct Proportion means the relationship between two values is fixed. If you double the quantity, you must double the value. They move in perfect sync.

Adv Year 2 Level "If 2 stickers cost 10p, how much do 6 stickers cost?"
Avg Year 4 Level "A recipe for 4 people uses 300g of sugar. How much sugar is needed for 10 people?"
Adv Year 5 Level (11+) "On a map with a scale of 1:25,000, a road is 8cm long. What is the actual length in kilometers?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Additive" Trap: If a recipe for 4 people is scaled to 6 people, students often see the "extra 2 people" and just add 2 to the ingredients.

The Fix: Scaling is a multiplication game, not an addition game. If you add instead of multiply, the "taste" (ratio) of the recipe changes.
