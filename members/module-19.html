Topic Logic: Solving "Missing Signs" is the reverse of calculation. It requires testing how operators transform a set of numbers into a Target Result, while strictly respecting BODMAS priority.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Left-to-Right" Trap: Forgetting that $\times$ and $\div$ must be tested first.
Example: In $12 - 4 \div 2$, a student might do $12-4=8$, then $8\div 2 = 4$. But BODMAS says $4 \div 2$ happens first!

The Fix: Always check if your second sign is "heavier" ($\times, \div$) than your first. If it is, solve that pair first before testing the balance.
