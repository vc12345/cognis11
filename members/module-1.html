The Unitary Method

Mastering the "Bridge" to scale any value.

Phase 01 The X-Ray

This method is hidden in any question that gives you a Group Value and asks for a New Total. You are looking for the "Bridge."

"If 8 pens cost £4.80, what do 5 pens cost?"
"12 crates weigh 144kg. Find the weight of 7."
"A 5-minute shower uses 40L. How much for 8 mins?"

Phase 04: The Saboteur

Watch out for the Inverse Slip. Students often divide the items by the price (6 ÷ £30 = 0.2) and get confused. Always divide the Value by the Quantity to find the price of one.
