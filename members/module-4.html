Module 4: Inverse Proportion

The "Work is Constant" Rule

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "System 1" brain thinks: "If I double the workers, I double the days." That's the trap. In reality, more people working means the job gets done faster. The numbers move in opposite directions.

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Think of work as a Fixed Area. If the job is to paint 20 walls, that number doesn't change. If you add more painters, each painter does less, and the time shrinks.

The Master Formula:
Workers × Days = Total Work Units (This number never changes!)
