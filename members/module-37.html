Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "First Word" Trap: Finding a letter that works for the first word and assuming it's the answer.

The Fix: Always test your candidate letter against the least common word in the set. If it doesn't fit the hardest word, it's not the key. The answer is the only letter that has 100% agreement across all three word-locks.
