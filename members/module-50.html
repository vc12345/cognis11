Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Slide" Fallacy. Thinking the hole simply moves to the other side of the paper while staying in the same relative position (e.g. always in the "top-left" of its section).

The Fix: Mirror Mapping. Every fold line is a mirror. If the hole is 15mm from the crease, its reflection must be exactly 15mm away on the opposite side. If you have two folds, apply this logic twice.
