Module 51: Block Counting

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Block counting is the study of Inferred Volume. You must assume the "Rule of Gravity": if a block is sitting at Level 3, there must be two hidden blocks underneath it. Instead of counting faces, count Vertical Columns to ensure no foundation block is missed.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Counting every visible square as a block. A corner block shows 3 faces, but it is still only 1 block.

The Fix: The Column Tally. Look at the top face of every "Skyscraper." Write down its height (e.g. 3 blocks). Move to the next. Summing the column heights is the only way to avoid the face-counting trap.
