Module 19: The Mean (Average)

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker confuses the Mean with the Median. A "Type 2" thinker remembers: **"Mean is Mean"** because it makes you do the most work (Adding and then Dividing).

1. "Find the mean of 4, 7, and 10."

2. "The mean of 4 numbers is 10. What is their total?"

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Step 1: Sum (Add all numbers together).
Step 2: Count (How many numbers are there?).
Step 3: Divide (Sum ÷ Count).
