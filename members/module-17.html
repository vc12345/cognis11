Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Clockwise" Trap: Students often try to reverse operations in the order they were written (Op 1 then Op 2).
The Fix: Think of it like dressing: You put on socks, then shoes. To reverse, you must take off the shoes first. The last operation done is the first one undone!
