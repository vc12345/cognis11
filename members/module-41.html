Phase 1: The X-Ray

In logical ordering, we are creating a relative hierarchy. Since we rarely know the exact position of an item, we must use clues to eliminate impossible sequences. By visualizing the items as tokens on a line, we can see which arrangements survive the logic of "Stronger to the Right."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Fixing an item in "First Place" as soon as a clue mentions it is "stronger," without checking if there is another item stronger still.

The Fix: Relative Anchoring. Instead of guessing a rank, use the clues to eliminate rows where the "Stronger" item is to the left of the "Weaker" one. The survivor is the answer.
