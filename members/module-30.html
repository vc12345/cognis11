Module 30: Reverse Thinking

Phase 1: The X-Ray

This is the "Number Machine" logic. When a question says "I think of a number...", you must travel backward from the result to the start by using opposite operations.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Forward" Trap: Trying to apply the operations to the result in the order they were written (e.g., adding 5 to 20). You MUST work from the end and do the opposite!
