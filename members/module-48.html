Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Face Inventory" Error. Choosing a net because it has 5 triangles, even though the solid only has 4.

The Fix: The Base Match. A prism always has 2 base shapes and N rectangles. A pyramid always has 1 base shape and N triangles. If the base has 8 sides (Octagon), look for exactly 8 side-flaps.
