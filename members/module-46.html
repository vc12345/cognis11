Module 46: Series Progression

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A non-verbal series is a visual pattern moving through time. To find the next frame, you must measure the Delta ($\Delta$)—the specific change between Frame 1 and Frame 2. If the dot moves 20 pixels right in Frame 2, it must move 20 pixels right in Frame 4.

Advanced Year 2

A circle alternates between the top and bottom of the frame.

Average Year 4

A shape rotates 90° clockwise and changes from Black to White.

Advanced Year 5

The "Triple Vector": Position, Rotation, and Size all change independently.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Visual Overload. Trying to "guess" the final image by looking at all three moving parts at once.

The Fix: Feature Isolation. Use your finger to cover the center of the frame and track only the corner elements. Once you have a rule for the corner, ignore it and look only at the center.
