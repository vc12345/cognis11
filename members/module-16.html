Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Split First" Trap: Taking the total (100) and dividing by 2 (50), then adding the gap (50 + 20 = 70).
The Result: 70 + 50 = 120. The total is broken!
The Fix: Level first! (Sum - Difference) ÷ 2. This ensures the total is preserved.
