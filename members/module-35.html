Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Edge Only" Trap: Most students only try putting the moved letter at the start or end of the new word.

The Fix: Treat the second word like a set of gaps: _ B _ I _ T _. The moved letter could hide in any of those four positions. If it doesn't work at the ends, start sliding it through the middle.
