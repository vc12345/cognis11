Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Missing Middle" Trap: When finding the Median, students often pick the middle number of the *original* list.

The Fix: You must Sort first. The Median is not the middle of the story; it is the middle of the line-up. Always line your data up from smallest to largest before looking for the center.
