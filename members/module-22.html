Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Inside-Out" Rule: The most common mistake is filling in the "Only" sections first.

The Fix: You must start at the core. Because the center group is part of everything, they have already been counted in the totals for A, B, and C. If you don't subtract them as you move outward, your total population will be far higher than reality.
