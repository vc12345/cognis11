Module 40: Odd One Out

Phase 1: The X-Ray

You do not need to read all 5 words to find the odd one out. By processing words from left to right, you can establish a majority rule as soon as you have seen two similar items. Once you have two of the same category and one different, the puzzle logic is "closed."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Reading all 5 words before starting to categorize. This is a linear processing habit that wastes time.

The Fix: The 2+1 Rule. As soon as you have 2 Majority words and 1 Outlier, the solution is confirmed. If words 1 and 2 match, and word 3 is different, word 3 is the answer. Do not read 4 or 5.
