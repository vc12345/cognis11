The Model

Ten minutes. In the same room.

Cognis11 is built to be led by you. Each module prepares you first, then gives you the tools to guide your child. Here is how a session works.

1

You read the X-Ray

Master the concept, the most common error, and the structural fix — in 60 seconds.

2

You guide the session

Walk your child through the interactive logic engine together, using your understanding to answer their questions.

3

The Lattice tracks progress

Each completed module updates the dashboard so you always know where to go next.

Why parent-led? Children under 11 build new mental models through guided dialogue with a trusted adult — not from passive screen time. The parent's presence is the mechanism by which the learning transfers. Cognis11 is designed around this.
