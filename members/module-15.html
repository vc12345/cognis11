Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Decimal" Trap: Writing 1 hour 30 mins as "1.3" or "1.30".

The Fix: Time doesn't reset at 100. It resets at 60. Always ask: "Does my minute total have a 60 hidden inside it?" If yes, pull it out and add it to the Hours column.
