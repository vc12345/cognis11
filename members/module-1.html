Phase 4: The Saboteur

  • The Inverse Slip: Dividing quantity by cost (e.g., 6 ÷ £60) instead of cost by quantity. Check: Is your unit price 10p or £10?
  • The Additive Trap: Thinking "I have 4 more items, so I'll just add 4 to the price." Ratios are multiplicative, never additive.
