Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Reading all 5 options before thinking. This wastes 40% of your available time on words that only confirm what you already know.

The Fix: The 3-Word Threshold. By word three, you must have a "Locked Rule." If words 1 and 2 match but 3 doesn't, word 3 is the outlier. If 1 and 3 match but 2 doesn't, word 2 is the outlier. STOP scanning once found.
