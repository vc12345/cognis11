Phase 1: The X-Ray

Intensity is the "volume control" of vocabulary. While two words might share a category, they carry different emotional and logical weights. Success requires matching the intensity of the word to the intensity of the situation.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

Choosing a synonym that is technically correct but "quiet" when the context is "loud." If a building is being destroyed, it isn't "damaged"—it's "demolished." Always check if the word's physical size in your mind matches the event.
