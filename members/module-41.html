Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Treating the items like a list rather than a map. If you only remember "who is next," you'll lose the gap where a third person might fit.

The Fix: The Elastic Line. Place the first person. Everyone else is either to their left or right. As the columns shrink, the line expands, showing you the true relative ranking without needing absolute numbers.
