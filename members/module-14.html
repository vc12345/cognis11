Module 14: Interval Logic

THE "PLUS ONE" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

This module exposes the "Simple Division" error. We assume that if we have a 10m fence with a post every 2m, we need 5 posts. We are forgetting the Starting Post at 0 meters.

Look for these wordings in the exam:
1. "A 20m road has lamp posts every 5m. How many posts are there?"
2. "A tree is planted every 3m along a 30m path. How many trees are needed?"
3. "A doctor sees a patient every 15 minutes for 1 hour. How many patients?"

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Look at your hand. You have 4 spaces between your fingers, but you have 5 fingers. In a straight line, there is always one more object than there are gaps.

The Formula:
(Total Length ÷ Gap Size) = Number of Gaps
Number of Gaps + 1 = Number of Posts
