Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Near-Miss" Trap: Choosing a word that is technically a synonym but lacks the correct intensity.

The Fix: Use the "Sentence Test." Replace the word in the sentence and see if it makes the meaning too weak or too dramatic. If someone is "ecstatic," saying they are "happy" is a near-miss; it's the right category, but the wrong intensity.
