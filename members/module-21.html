Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Double-Count" Trap: If a question says "15 people like Pizza and 10 like both," many students put 15 in the "Pizza Only" section.

The Fix: The group total includes the overlap. To find the "Only" section, you must subtract the "Both" section. Always fill the middle of the Venn diagram first!
