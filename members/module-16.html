System 2 Master Module 16

The "More Than" Trap

Turning a speed-trap into a logical certainty.

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Type 2 minds don't look at numbers first. They look at intent. These three questions are identical skeletons wearing different clothes.

A: Total: £120. Coat is £100 more than Hat. Hat = ?
B: Total: 50 kids. 10 more Boys than Girls. Girls = ?
C: Total: 80cm. A is 20cm longer than B. B = ?

Phase 2: The Anatomy

The "System 1 Snap" tells you to just subtract the difference. That is the trap. If you take the extra away, you are left with two equal parts.

Phase 3: The Engine

Adjust the sliders to build your own 11+ question. See how the logic holds up regardless of the scale.

A total of 100 items were found. There were 20 more of Item A than Item B. How many of Item B were there?
Logic: (100 - 20) = 80. Half of 80 is 40. Smallest = 40.
