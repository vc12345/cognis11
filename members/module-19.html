Module 19: The Mean

THE "BALANCE" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker confuses Mean with Median. A "Type 2" thinker knows that the Mean is the "Share Fairly" result—what everyone would have if we pooled everything and divided it equally.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "Calculate the mean of 5, 8, 12, and 15."
2. "The mean weight of 4 boxes is 10kg. What is their total weight?"
3. "Find the average score of the team."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Step 1: Sum - Add all the values together.
Step 2: Count - Count how many values there are.
Step 3: Divide - Total Sum ÷ Count = Mean.
