Module 16v2: The "More Than" Trap

THE "TAKE AWAY THEN SHARE" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker sees two numbers and just halves the total. A "Type 2" thinker knows that if one person has more, you must remove that extra amount from the total before you divide it between them.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "Sam and Alice have 20 sweets. Sam has 4 more than Alice. How many does Alice have?"
2. "A book and a pen cost £11. The book is £10 more than the pen. Find the price of the pen."
3. "The sum of two numbers is 30. Their difference is 6. Find the smaller number."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Step 1: Total Amount - The Difference = Remaining Total.
Step 2: Remaining Total ÷ 2 = The Smaller Share.
Step 3: Smaller Share + Difference = The Larger Share.
