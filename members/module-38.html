Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Choosing a synonym that is technically correct but "quiet" when the context is "loud" (e.g. saying a fire was "warm" instead of "scorching").

The Fix: The "Sentence Volume" test. Look for the word whose intensity matches the drama of the event. If the event is extreme, choose the word that sits at the far right of the thermal scale.
