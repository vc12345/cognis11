Module 21: Pie Chart Angles

THE "MAGIC 360" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker confuses people with degrees. A "Type 2" thinker knows that the circle is a fixed $360^\circ$ and uses the **Multiplier** to convert frequency into angles.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "Show this data in a pie chart."
2. "Calculate the angle for the 'Red' category."
3. "If 10 people are represented by $60^\circ$, how many people in total?"

Phase 2: The Anatomy

The Formula:
(Value ÷ Total Frequency) × 360 = Angle in Degrees.
