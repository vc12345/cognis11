The human entry point.

Cognis11 was founded on a simple observation: the most capable children are often the most poorly served by standard 11+ preparation — because drilling ignores the need for dialogue.

We adapt Schema-Based Instruction (SBI)—a pillar of cognitive psychology studied for over thirty years. While SBI has traditionally been a remedial framework, we explore its potential for high-performance optimization.

By training the brain to classify the logical "skeleton" of a problem before attempting the solution, we reduce the cognitive load that causes high-ability reasoners to slow down.

The Parent-Led Mandate

Schema transfer in children under 11 is a socially-mediated process. Children do not build new mental models from passive screen time; they build them through interaction with a trusted adult.

Cognis11 is designed to be led by you. The module prepares the parent in 60 seconds, then provides the interactive tools to guide the child. Ten minutes of shared insight beats an hour of isolated drilling.

Our motivation lies in the hope that education can be more than just a game of peer ranking. We want children to develop genuine structural understanding—and for those 10 minutes a day to be a moment of shared discovery.

The Founding Team

Our team brings together academia (cognitive neuroscience), financial analysis, and digital product building. We pointed these distinct backgrounds at the 11+ curriculum to see if we could build a better entry point for the "Deep Understander."
