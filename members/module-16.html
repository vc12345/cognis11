Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: To find two unknown values when given a Sum and a Difference, we must equalize the values by "snipping off" the excess gap. This leaves two identical parts that can be divided perfectly.

Phase 4: The Saboteur — The "Type 1" Impulse

The Trap: Solving as 100 ÷ 2 = 50, then saying the shares are 50 and 70 (50+20).
The Error: 70 + 50 = 120. You've increased the total by mistake!
The Fix: Level first. Subtract the gap (100 - 20 = 80), then divide (80 ÷ 2 = 40). The shares are 40 and 60. Total = 100. Correct!
