Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Symmetry Neglect. Thinking the hole stays in the same coordinate relative to the *top* edge, rather than the *fold* edge.

The Fix: Mirror Mapping. If the hole is 2cm away from the fold line, its reflection **must** be exactly 2cm away on the other side. Always unfold one step at a time in reverse order.
