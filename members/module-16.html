Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Type 1" Impulse:

Dividing the total by 2 first, then adding the difference. If Total is 10 and Gap is 2, Type 1 logic says: 10 ÷ 2 = 5. Then 5 + 2 = 7. But 7 + 5 = 12, not 10!

The "Cognis" Fix: Always level the playing field. Subtract the difference first to create two equal blocks, then divide. This ensures the total stays consistent.
