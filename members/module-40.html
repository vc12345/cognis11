Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Completion" Trap: Reading all 5 words even when you've already found the answer at word 3.

The Fix: Use the 3-Word Rule. If word 1 and 2 follow Rule A, and word 3 follows Rule B, one of those first three is the answer. Check word 4 only to confirm. Once confirmed, STOP.
