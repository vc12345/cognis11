Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Decimal" Trap: Writing 1 hour 30 mins as "1.3" or "1.30".

The Fix: Time is Base-60. If the minutes column reaches 60, it resets and "carries" 1 to the hours. Never treat it like a decimal point!
