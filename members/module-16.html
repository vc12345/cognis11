Phase 1: The X-Ray

The "More Than" Trap asks you to find values for two objects when you are given their Total (Sum) and the Gap (Difference) between them.

EntryTwo friends have 12 stickers. Sarah has 4 more than Tom. How many does Tom have?
StandardA tablet and a case cost £250. The tablet is £200 more than the case. Find the cost of the case.
AdvancedThe perimeter of a rectangle is 56cm. The length is 8cm longer than the width. Calculate the area.

Phase 4: The Saboteur — The "Type 1" Impulse

The Trap: Dividing the total by 2 first, then adding the difference. If Total is 10 and Gap is 2, Type 1 logic says 10 ÷ 2 = 5, then 5 + 2 = 7. But 7 + 5 = 12, not 10!

The "Cognis" Fix: Level the playing field first. Subtract the difference to create two equal blocks, then divide. This ensures the total is preserved.
