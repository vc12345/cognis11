Module 17: Reverse Operations

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: Reverse Operations (or Inverse functions) require us to work backward from a known result to find a hidden starting point. Every action must be undone in **reverse order** using the **opposite operation**.

Advanced Y2"I think of a number. I add 5 and get 12. What was my number?"
Average Y4"I think of a number. I multiply by 3 then subtract 4. The result is 20. Find my number."
Advanced Y5"A number is divided by 4, then 7 is added. The result is then squared to get 81. What was the number?"

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Clockwise" Trap: Undoing operations in the order they were written (reversing Op 1 then Op 2).
The Fix: Last In, First Out. Imagine dressing: Socks, then Shoes. To reverse, you must take off the shoes first. Reverse the last operation first!
