Module 40: Odd One Out

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Odd One Out is a game of **Majority Rule**. You aren't looking for the "weird" word; you are looking for the word that breaks the rule shared by at least three others. Speed comes from finding the rule early.

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: Reading all 5 options slowly. In time-pressured exams, every second spent reading word #5 after finding the answer is a lost mark elsewhere.

The Fix: The 3-Word Rule. Once three words agree on a category, scan for the one that disagrees. As soon as you find it, STOP. Don't read the rest.
