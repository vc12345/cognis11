Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Digit Gluing" Trap: If A = 5, many students see 3A and think the answer is 35.

The Fix: In algebra, when a number and letter touch, they are multiplied. Think of the letter as a container. You aren't putting the number next to the 3; you are pouring the value into the container: 3 × (5) = 15.
