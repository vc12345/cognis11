Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Inverse Slip": The most common mistake is dividing the wrong way. If 5 items cost £20, the unit price is £20 ÷ 5, not 5 ÷ 20.

The Fix: Always ask yourself: "Am I looking for the price of one item, or the 'item-ness' of one pound?" 99% of the time, you want the price of the item.
