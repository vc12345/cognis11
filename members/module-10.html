Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Unit Mismatch" Trap: The most common error is ignoring units. If speed is in km per hour but the time is given in minutes, you cannot simply multiply. You must convert the minutes into hours first.

The Fix: Always write the units next to your numbers. If they don't match (e.g. miles and km), stop and convert to a single "Unit Language" before calculating.
