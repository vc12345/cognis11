Module 7: Area vs Perimeter

THE "FENCE VS GRASS" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker mixes up the two formulas. A "Type 2" thinker visualizes the difference: Perimeter is the Walk around the edge; Area is the Space inside.

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "How much fencing is needed for a garden 10m by 5m?" (Perimeter)
2. "How many tiles are needed to cover a floor 4m by 6m?" (Area)
3. "Find the total distance around the edge of a rectangle." (Perimeter)

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Area: Length × Width (Units squared)
Perimeter: (Length + Width) × 2 (Standard units)

Phase 3: The Engine

Area (Space): cm²

Perimeter (Edge): cm
