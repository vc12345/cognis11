Module 18: BODMAS (Order of Ops)

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: Mathematics isn't always read left-to-right. We use Operational Hierarchy. Operations like Multiplication and Division have a higher "energy" or priority than Addition and Subtraction, so they must be resolved first regardless of their position.

Entry (Year 2)"What is 2 + 3 × 5?"
Standard (Year 4)"Solve: 50 - (10 + 5) × 2."
Advanced (Year 5)"Insert brackets to make this true: 5 + 3 × 10 - 2 = 32."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Reading Direction" Trap: Solving the equation from left to right like a sentence.
Example: In 2 + 3 × 10, a Type 1 thinker does 2+3=5, then 5×10=50.

The Fix: Always scan for Brackets, then Orders, then DM. Only when those are gone do you settle the AS. The high-priority operations are "heavier" and must be settled first.
