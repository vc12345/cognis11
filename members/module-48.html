Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Face Inventory" Error. Choosing a net that has the right number of faces, but the wrong shapes (e.g., a prism with 5 squares instead of 3 rectangles and 2 triangles).

The Fix: The Perimeter Match. Count the sides of the "Base" shape. Every side of the base must have exactly one face attached to it in the final 3D form.
