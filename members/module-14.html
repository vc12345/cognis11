Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Fence Post" Trap: If a 20m fence has posts every 5m, students often say 4 posts ($20 \div 5$).

The Fix: $20 \div 5$ gives you the number of gaps. You need an extra post at the very start (the "Zero" position). So, $4 \text{ gaps} + 1 = 5 \text{ posts}$.
