Module 17: Reverse Operations

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: Reverse Operations (Inverse functions) are like retracing your steps. To find where you started, you must walk backwards from the end, doing the exact opposite of every instruction in the opposite order.

Entry (Year 2)"I think of a number. I add 5 and get 12. What was my number?"
Average (Year 4)"I multiply a number by 3, then subtract 4. The result is 20. What was it?"
Advanced (Year 5)"I square a number, add 4, then divide by 2 to get 10. Find the number."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Clockwise" Trap: Attempting to reverse Op 1 before Op 2 because it was written first.
The Fix: Last In, First Out (LIFO). Think of putting on socks then shoes. To reverse, you must take off the shoes first!
