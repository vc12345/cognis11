Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "One-Track" Trap: Students often apply the first rule they find to the entire word.

The Fix: Before shifting anything, "tag" every letter as either a Vowel or a Consonant. Only once the word is sorted can you safely apply the math. If you don't sort first, you'll miss the pivot point where the rule changes.
