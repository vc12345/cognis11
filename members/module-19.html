Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Linear" Trap: Most students test signs from left to right.
Example: In $10 + 2 \times 5$, a student might think it's $12 \times 5 = 60$. But it is $10 + 10 = 20$.

The Fix: If you pick a "heavy" sign (× or ÷) for your second slot, you must calculate that pair first. The scale handles the weight, but you must handle the order!
