Module 24: Translation

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Translation is a "slide." The shape does not turn, flip, or change size. It simply moves from one spot to another based on a Vector.

"Translate shape A by the vector $\begin{pmatrix} 3 \\ -2 \end{pmatrix}$."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

Vectors are written as $\begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \end{pmatrix}$. The top number ($x$) moves it left/right. The bottom number ($y$) moves it up/down.

Phase 3: The Engine

Vector: $\begin{pmatrix} \\ \end{pmatrix}$

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Inversion" Trap: Moving 3 left when the vector says 3 (positive). Always double-check your signs!
