The Design Principle

You are the teacher. We are the preparation.

Children under 11 don't acquire new conceptual frameworks from a screen. They acquire them from a person — through guided dialogue and shared attention.

Cognis11 is built around this reality. Every module prepares you first. In 60 seconds, you master the structural "X-Ray" of a concept. Then you guide your child through the interactive logic engine together. Ten minutes, in the same room, building a foundation that makes drilling actually effective.

Clarity for them. Efficiency for you.

Cognis11 is designed to be complementary to your current path, providing the structural classification skills that workbooks often miss.
