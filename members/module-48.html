Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Floating Flap." Picking a net where the side faces are not attached to the edges of the base.

The Fix: Edge Counting. If the base has 4 sides, there must be 4 flaps. If the base is a hexagon, there must be 6. Always count the perimeter of the main base first.
