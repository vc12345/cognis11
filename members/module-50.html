Phase 4: The Saboteur

The Trap: The "Asymmetric Slide." Thinking the hole simply moves to the other side without mirroring its position (Translation instead of Reflection).

The Fix: The Crease Ruler. If the punch is 2cm from the fold, its twin must be 2cm from the fold on the other side. Always work backwards from the last fold to the first.
