Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Left-to-Right" Trap: Linear thinking is the enemy.
Example: $2 + 3^2$. A Type 1 thinker does $2+3 = 5$, then $5^2 = 25$.

The Fix: Orders (^) and Multiplication/Division are physically heavier. They pull the focus first! In $2 + 3^2$, the $3^2$ (9) is solved first, making the total 11.
