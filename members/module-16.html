Module 16: The "More Than" Trap

Phase 1: The X-Ray

Topic Logic: When finding two values where you only know their Sum and their Difference, the goal is to "level the playing field." We remove the extra amount (the gap) so we are left with two identical shares that can be easily divided by 2.

Entry (Year 2)"Sarah and Jo have 10 sweets. Sarah has 2 more than Jo. How many does Jo have?"
Standard (Year 4)"A hat and a coat cost £70. The coat costs £30 more than the hat. What is the hat's price?"
Advanced (Year 5)"The perimeter of a rectangle is 48cm. The length is 4cm longer than the width. Find the area."

Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Half First" Trap: Most students take the total (100) and divide by 2 (50), then add the gap (50 + 20 = 70).
The Result: 70 + 50 = 120. The sum is now wrong!
The Fix: Level first. (Sum - Difference) ÷ 2. This ensures the total is preserved while finding the smaller share.
