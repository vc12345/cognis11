Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Type 1" Impulse:

Solving as $100 \div 2 = 50$, then saying the shares are $50$ and $70$.
The Error: $70 + 50 = 120$. You've increased the total by mistake!

The Fix: Level first. Subtract the gap ($100 - 20 = 80$), then divide ($80 \div 2 = 40$). The shares are $40$ and $60$.
