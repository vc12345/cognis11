Module 15: Venn Overlap

THE "SUM VS TOTAL" PRINCIPLE

Phase 1: The X-Ray

A "Type 1" thinker adds the groups together and assumes that's the total. A "Type 2" thinker knows that if the groups add up to more than the total people, that extra amount must be the people counted twice (the overlap).

Exam Wording Patterns:
1. "In a class of 30, 20 like Pizza and 15 like Burger. How many like both?"
2. "Of 50 students, 40 play Tennis and 25 play Football. How many play both?"
3. "12 children study French, 10 study Spanish. Total class size is 18. Find the overlap."

Phase 2: The Anatomy

The Formula:
(Group A + Group B) - Total Students = Overlap (Both)
