The Design Principle

You are the teacher. We are the preparation.

Children under 11 don't acquire new conceptual frameworks from a screen. They acquire them from a person — through guided dialogue with someone who can read the moment of confusion and respond to it.

Every module prepares you first. In 60 seconds, you understand the concept, the most common error, and the structural fix. Then you guide your child through the interactive logic engine together. No maths background required. No tutor needed.

Ten minutes. In the same room. That is the model — and it is not a convenience feature. It is the reason the learning works.
