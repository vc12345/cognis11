Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Decimal" Trap: Most students write 1 hour 30 mins as "1.3" or "1.30" because they are used to base-10 math.

The Fix: Time is Base-60. When the minutes container fills to 60, it spills over into the hours container. Always treat the ":" like a barrier, not a decimal point.
