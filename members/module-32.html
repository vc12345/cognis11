Phase 4: The Saboteur

The "Shape Blindness" Trap: Trying to "sound out" symbols as letters.

The Fix: Ignore what the symbol *looks* like. Instead, look at where it *lives*. If a symbol is at the start and end of a word (like Ψ _ Ψ), only look for words with matching first and last letters (like POP, DID, or EVE). Logic beats guessing every time.
